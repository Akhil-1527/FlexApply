About FlexApply

Our Story

FlexApply was founded with a simple mission: to help overwhelmed international students and working professionals save valuable time in their job search.

We understand the unique challenges visa holders face. Our team of experts has helped hundreds of OPT, CPT, STEM OPT, H1B, and GC EAD holders secure interviews with top companies by handling the tedious application process for them.

Rather than spending countless hours applying to jobs, our clients can focus on what truly matters - preparing for interviews and networking with potential employers.
