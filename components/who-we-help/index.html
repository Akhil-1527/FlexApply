Who We Help

Our job application service is designed for professionals facing specific challenges in the job market.

International Students (OPT/STEM OPT)

Time-sensitive job search with visa deadlines. Need employment within limited timeframes.

Common Challenge:

"I'm qualified but running out of time on my OPT/STEM OPT period."

Our Solution:

High-volume applications to OPT-friendly employers with proven sponsorship history.

H-1B Visa Holders

Specialized job search requiring employers willing to transfer or maintain visa sponsorship.

Common Challenge:

"Companies reject me as soon as they learn I need visa sponsorship."

Our Solution:

Targeted applications to H-1B transfer-friendly companies with custom cover letters addressing sponsorship proactively.

U.S. Citizens & Residents

Frustrated by the modern application process and low response rates despite qualifications.

Common Challenge:

"I've applied to 200+ jobs with no response. What am I doing wrong?"

Our Solution:

Strategic, high-volume applications with ATS optimization and personalized follow-up sequences.

Career Changers

Struggling to get interviews when transitioning to a new industry or role.

Common Challenge:

"I have transferable skills but can't get past the application filters."

Our Solution:

Skill-focused applications with targeted cover letters that highlight relevant transferable experience.
